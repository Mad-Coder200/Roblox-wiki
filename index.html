Devil Fruit

There are currently ... devil fruits in the game

What are devil fruits?

Devil Fruits in King Legacy are very important to have for both grinding and PVP. Devil Fruits are fruits that, when eaten, will give the user an ability that can fall under any of the three categories: Paramecia, Zoan, and Logia. The only disadvantage of eating a Devil Fruit is that the user will lose the ability to swim, so other forms of transportation, like boats, flying, or ice paths, will be needed to travel long distances.

Gacha

  • Gacha is one of the easiest ways to obtain a Devil Fruit, as you only need 250,000 Beli to acquire 1 with a maximum of 5 (1,250,000 Beli) and 7 (1,750,000 Beli) for premium players every 6 hours.
  • You can also obtain a Devil Fruit by spending 1 Gem. You can buy as much Devil Fruits as you want as long as you have enough Gems, because there is no limit to how much you can spend if you use it.

Black Market

  • Black Market allows you to procure Devil Fruits with its system which cycles through different Devil Fruits per hour.
  • You can purchase a Devil Fruit by using Beli and Gems. You can also purchase a Devil Fruit by using Robux, which gives you permanent access to the Devil Fruit you purchased any time.

Spawn

  • Devil Fruits can spawn under trees everywhere in the map every hour, and despawns every 30 minutes.
  • The least efficient method of acquiring Devil Fruits since there are 2 other simpler and easier ways to obtain one other than relying on luck or Fruit Position .

Legacy Island

  • Every hour in Second Sea (Server time), a Legacy Island spawns with a Sea King surrounding it.
  • After defeating the Sea King , a cave will open, which has a Treasure Chest inside. Once you've claimed the Treasure Chest, it will reward you with a Devil Fruit and other benefits.

Fighting Stlye

There are currently 6 fighting style in the game

What are Fighting style?

Fighting stlyes are different techniques that replace melee when learnt and uses the melee stat point. Buso can be used to increase damage of fighting stlye and fighting style can also be known as martial arts in game.

Melee

  • This is the worst fighting stlye in game and also the fighting stlye a beginner spawns with (worst fighting style).

Black Leg

  • Black leg can be bought for 150 000$ in the rocks island.
  • This fighting stlye changes melee attacks from hands to the leg and also it changes the position of the buso haki to the legs. This fighting stlye is good for travel and is the 2nd most used fighting in the game. With the use of Diable Jamble, damage increases and has flame effect on attacks.

Cyborg

  • Cyborg can be bought for 1 million in shark island under a tree.
  • This fighting stlye make player a half robot. This is most used fighting stlye in the game mainly because of its healing capability and it is also good for pvp. It mainly middle ranged moves and 1 AOE movess that takes nearly 90% of health.

Fishman Karate

  • It can be bought for 1.3million in fishman island.
  • This is second worst fighting stlye b'cuz it is bad for almost everything and it is the 2nd least used in the game. This fighting stlye allows on to uses water as a medium of attack.

Dragon Claw

  • It can be bought for 1.25million in second sea.
  • This fighting allows the player to use fire as a medium of attack, it is also the best for close range battle. Though, it is underrated but very high damage.

Electric

  • It can be bought for 2.5million in second sea.
  • This fighting allows the player to use electric as a medium of attack, it is also the best fighting style in the game and the 3rd most used. Best for pvp, raids, grinding and has really fast attacks.
